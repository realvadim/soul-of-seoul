Introduction
Seoul International Fireworks Festival is an annual event of extravagant firework displays by firework masters from home and abroad. Prior to the firework show, visitors will be able to enjoy various performances and programs. This year, the show will start at 19:30, and feature firework displays by professional teams from home and abroad.
